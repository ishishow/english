## 10.1 致命的エラー：時制の混乱

### 現在形 vs 過去形の混同

```
❌ Bad: "I go to I went to lunch"
✅ Good: "I went to lunch"

❌ Bad: "when we became become adult"
✅ Good: "when we become adults" / "when we became adults"

❌ Bad: "when I was when we were child, when we are child"
✅ Good: "when we were children" / "when I was a child"
```

**ルール**: 過去の話は過去形で統一、一般論は現在形で統一

| 場面 | 正しい表現 |
|------|-----------|
| 今日カフェに行った | I went to a cafe today |
| 子供の頃 | when I was a child |
| 大人になると（一般論） | when we become adults |
| 大人になった時（過去） | when I became an adult |

## 10.2 致命的エラー：可算名詞の複数形

```
❌ Bad: "when we were child"
✅ Good: "when we were children"

❌ Bad: "50 member"
✅ Good: "50 members"

❌ Bad: "a lot of thing"
✅ Good: "a lot of things"

❌ Bad: "two hour"
✅ Good: "two hours"
```

**ルール**: 数詞や many/a lot of の後は**複数形**

| 数 | 正しい表現 |
|------|-----------|
| 50人 | 50 members / 50 people |
| 2時間 | two hours |
| たくさんのこと | a lot of things / many things |
| 1.5時間 | 1.5 hours / one and a half hours |

## 10.3 重要エラー：冠詞の欠如

```
❌ Bad: "I went to lunch, some cafe"
✅ Good: "I went to lunch at a cafe"

❌ Bad: "it is good for our health body"
✅ Good: "it is good for our body and health"

✅ Good: "I am a sports player"

❌ Bad: "playing baseball game"
```

**ルール**: 職業・役割には **a/an** が必要

## 10.4 重要エラー：前置詞の誤用

```
❌ Bad: "happiness from simple thing like such as food"
✅ Good: "happiness from simple things such as food"

❌ Bad: "I moved only from my family"
✅ Good: "I moved away from my family"

❌ Bad: "it is very near from my house"
✅ Good: "it is very close to my hometown" / "my house is very near"
```

### near vs close の使い分け

| 表現 | 正しい使い方 |
|------|-----------|
| near | near + 名詞（前置詞不要）: "near my house" |
| close | close to + 名詞: "close to my house" |
| from | from は「〜から離れる」: "far from", "away from" |

## 10.7 新出イディオム・表現

### "Live your life to the fullest"
**意味**: 人生を精一杯生きる、後悔なく生きる
```
例: Don't overthink. Live your life to the fullest.
    考えすぎないで。人生を精一杯生きて。
```

### "Don't take everything personally"
**意味**: 何事も個人的に受け取りすぎない
```
例: He criticized my work, but I try not to take it personally.
    彼は私の仕事を批判したが、個人的に受け取らないようにしている。
```

### "get along with ~"
**意味**: 〜と仲良くやっている
```
例: I get along well with my roommates.
    ルームメイトとはうまくやっている。
```

## 10.11 練習問題 解答

### A. 時制を正しく直せ
1. Yesterday I **went** to the mountain.
2. When I **was a child**, I **liked** baseball.
3. I **have lived** here for three years now.
4. When we **become adults**, we understand more things.

### B. 複数形に直せ
1. I have two **hours** before the meeting.
2. There are 50 **members** in our share house.
3. I can think of many **reasons**.
4. Children feel happy from simple **things**.

### C. 冠詞を入れよ
1. I am **a** software engineer.
2. She became **an** adult last year.
3. I went to **a** cafe with my friend.
4. He is **a** sports player.

### D. 日本語を英語に
1. I played baseball when I was a child.
2. Money is not a requirement for happiness. / Money isn't necessary for happiness.
3. You should live your life to the fullest.
4. I get along well with my roommates.

## 10.12 総合評価

| 項目 | 評価 | コメント |
|------|------|---------|
| 時制の正確さ | D | 現在形と過去形が頻繁に混在 |
| 冠詞の使用 | D | ほぼ全ての場面で欠如 |
| 複数形 | D | 数詞の後も単数形を使用 |
| 語彙力 | C | 基本的な単語は使えている |
| 流暢さ | C | 言い直しが多く、詰まることが多い |
| 内容の深さ | B | 自分の意見をしっかり述べている |

### 次回までの課題
1. **可算名詞の複数形を徹底する** - 特に数詞の後
2. **時制を統一する** - 過去の話は過去形、一般論は現在形
3. **冠詞の練習** - 職業・役割には必ず a/an
4. **新出イディオムを使う** - "Live your life to the fullest" を日常で使ってみる
