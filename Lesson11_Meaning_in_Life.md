# Lesson 11: Meaning in Life【人生の意味】

## レッスン概要
- **トピック**: 人生の意味、幸福、宗教観、経験
- **評価**: C（基礎に問題あり）
- **主な課題**: 前置詞の誤用、時制の混乱、冠詞の欠如、不完全な文

---

## 11.1 致命的エラー：前置詞の誤用

### believe の正しい使い方

```
❌ Bad: "I don't believe God"
✅ Good: "I don't believe in God"

❌ Bad: "I believe about the evolution"
✅ Good: "I believe in evolution"
```

**ルール**: believe の後に「〜の存在を信じる」場合は **believe in** を使う

| 表現 | 意味 | 例文 |
|------|------|------|
| believe + 人/物 | 〜の言葉を信じる | I believe you (あなたの言うことを信じる) |
| believe in + 人/物 | 〜の存在・価値を信じる | I believe in God (神の存在を信じる) |
| believe that + 文 | 〜だと思う | I believe that he is honest |

---

## 11.2 致命的エラー：apply の正しい使い方

```
❌ Bad: "I applied English agent"
✅ Good: "I applied to an English travel agency"

❌ Bad: "I submit"
✅ Good: "I applied" / "I submitted an application"
```

**ルール**: apply は **apply to + 場所** または **apply for + 職/プログラム**

| 表現 | 意味 |
|------|------|
| apply to + 会社/学校 | 〜に応募する |
| apply for + 仕事/ビザ | 〜を申請する |

## 11.3 重要エラー：不完全な文構造

```
❌ Bad: "It's very happy for me"
✅ Good: "It makes me very happy" / "I feel very happy"

❌ Bad: "I studied to learn English"
✅ Good: "I've been studying English" / "I started learning English"

❌ Bad: "This is my first abroad travel"
✅ Good: "This was my first trip abroad"
```

### 形容詞 happy の正しい使い方

| パターン | 例文 |
|----------|------|
| 人 + be + happy | I am happy |
| It makes + 人 + happy | It makes me happy |
| It is + 形容詞 + for 人 + to do | It is important for me to learn |

**注意**: "It's happy for me" は不自然。happy は人の感情なので主語は人。

---

## 11.4 重要エラー：時制の混乱

```
❌ Bad: "I studied native camp this December, and I will be continuing"
✅ Good: "I started taking Native Camp lessons this December, and I will continue"

❌ Bad: "I studied snowboarding or playing golf"
✅ Good: "I started learning snowboarding and playing golf"

❌ Bad: "I didn't, and I can't speak English"
✅ Good: "I couldn't speak English at all"
```

### study vs learn vs start の使い分け

| 動詞 | 意味 | 例文 |
|------|------|------|
| study | 勉強する（行為） | I study English every day |
| learn | 習得する（結果） | I'm learning to speak English |
| start + ~ing | 〜し始める | I started learning English |

---

## 11.5 重要エラー：冠詞の欠如

```
❌ Bad: "I went to Malta Europe"
✅ Good: "I went to Malta in Europe"

❌ Bad: "as a country"
✅ Good: "to a foreign country" / "abroad"

❌ Bad: "This is my first abroad travel"
✅ Good: "This was my first trip abroad"
```

## 11.8 新出イディオム・表現

### "be driven by ~"
**意味**: 〜に駆り立てられる、〜が原動力となる
```
例: Most people's lives are driven by money and family.
    多くの人の人生はお金と家族によって動かされている。
```

### "make sense"
**意味**: 意味をなす、理解できる
```
例: That doesn't make sense to me.
    それは私には理解できません。

例: Now it makes sense.
    今なら納得できる。
```

### "no offense"
**意味**: 悪気はない（失礼なことを言う前のクッション言葉）
```
例: No offense, but children can be selfish.
    悪気はないけど、子供は自己中心的になりがちだ。
```

## 11.9 日本の宗教観を説明する表現

```
We Japanese have a unique religious culture.
日本人は独特の宗教文化を持っています。

We celebrate Christmas and Halloween, but most of us don't believe in Christianity.
私たちはクリスマスやハロウィンを祝いますが、キリスト教を信じている人は少ないです。

On New Year's Day, we visit shrines or temples to pray.
正月には神社やお寺に参拝します。

It might seem strange, but that's Japanese culture.
奇妙に見えるかもしれませんが、それが日本文化です。
```

## 11.12 練習問題

### A. 前置詞を入れよ

1. I don't believe ___ ghosts.
2. She applied ___ the university.
3. He applied ___ a scholarship.
4. I went ___ Malta ___ Europe.

### B. 正しい形に直せ

1. It's very happy for me to learn English.
2. I studied to learn snowboarding this year.
3. This is my first abroad travel.
4. I didn't speak English not at all.

1. 多くの人は家族とお金に駆り立てられている
2. 私は神を信じていません
3. 正月に神社に行きます
4. それは意味をなさない

1. "I reach 85 lesson"
2. "we have only 10 days"
3. "children are selfish in a good way"

## 11.13 練習問題 解答

### A. 前置詞を入れよ
1. I don't believe **in** ghosts.
2. She applied **to** the university.
3. He applied **for** a scholarship.
4. I went **to** Malta **in** Europe.

### B. 正しい形に直せ
1. **Learning English makes me very happy.** / **I'm very happy to learn English.**
2. **I started learning snowboarding this year.**
3. **This was my first trip abroad.**
4. **I couldn't speak English at all.**

### C. 日本語を英語に
1. Most people are driven by family and money.
2. I don't believe in God.
3. I visit a shrine on New Year's Day.
4. That doesn't make sense.

### D. 自然な表現に直せ
1. "I've completed 85 lessons" / "I've reached lesson 85"
2. "We only have 10 days left"
3. "Children are innocently self-centered" / "Children are naturally focused on themselves"

---

## 11.14 総合評価

| 項目 | 評価 | コメント |
|------|------|---------|
| 前置詞の正確さ | D | believe in, apply to/for が使えていない |
| 時制の正確さ | D | 過去と現在が混在 |
| 文構造 | D | 不完全な文が多い |
| 語彙力 | C+ | curiosity, responsibility など使えている |
| 内容の深さ | B+ | 宗教観など深い話題に挑戦している |
| コミュニケーション | B | 伝えようとする姿勢が良い |

### 次回までの課題
1. **believe in** を使う練習 - 存在を信じる場合は in が必要
2. **apply to/for** の使い分け - 場所には to、物には for
3. **文を完結させる** - 主語＋動詞＋目的語の基本構造を守る
4. **"make sense"** を日常会話で使ってみる
