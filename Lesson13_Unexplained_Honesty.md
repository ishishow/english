# Lesson 13: The Unexplained & Honesty【不可解な現象と誠実さ】

## レッスン概要
- **トピック**: 説明できない現象、宗教、占い、誠実さ
- **評価**: C（基礎に問題あり）
- **主な課題**: 動詞の使い方、前置詞の欠如、不完全な文

---

## 13.1 致命的エラー：pray の正しい使い方

```
❌ Bad: "to play a means believing Buddhism"
✅ Good: "to pray means believing in Buddhism"

❌ Bad: "we go to shrine to play"
✅ Good: "we go to shrine to pray"
```

**注意**: play（遊ぶ）と pray（祈る）は全く違う単語！

| 単語 | 発音 | 意味 |
|------|------|------|
| play /pleɪ/ | プレイ | 遊ぶ、演奏する |
| pray /preɪ/ | プレイ | 祈る |

発音はほぼ同じだが、スペルと意味が異なる！

---

## 13.2 致命的エラー：believe in の欠如

```
❌ Bad: "we believe many gods"
✅ Good: "we believe in many gods"

❌ Bad: "we believe Buddhism"
✅ Good: "we believe in Buddhism"
```

**ルール**: 宗教・神・存在を信じる場合は **believe in** を使う

---

## 13.3 重要エラー：動詞の形

### observe の正しい使い方

```
❌ Bad: "they are good at observe"
✅ Good: "they are good at observing"

❌ Bad: "good at observe being a person"
✅ Good: "good at observing people"
```

**ルール**: 前置詞 (at, in, of, about) の後は **動名詞 (-ing)**

---

## 13.4 重要エラー：語順と文構造

```
❌ Bad: "my experience, all my experience, I think"
✅ Good: "I think all my experiences can be explained"

❌ Bad: "I haven't experienced the strange things"
✅ Good: "I haven't experienced anything strange"

❌ Bad: "I did same situation"
✅ Good: "I was in the same situation" / "I experienced the same situation"
```

---

## 13.5 重要エラー：advise vs advice

```
❌ Bad: "they can advise them very effective"
✅ Good: "they can advise them very effectively"

❌ Bad: "they can advise effectively"
✅ Good: "they can give effective advice"
```

| 単語 | 品詞 | 意味 |
|------|------|------|
| advise /ədˈvaɪz/ | 動詞 | アドバイスする |
| advice /ədˈvaɪs/ | 名詞 | アドバイス |

**注意**: advice は不可算名詞（an advice ❌, some advice ✅）

## 13.9 新出イディオム・表現

### "come true"
**意味**: 実現する、本当になる
```
例: Have you ever had a dream that came true?
    正夢を見たことがありますか？
```

### "fall apart"
**意味**: 崩壊する、バラバラになる
```
例: Without honesty, the world would fall apart.
    誠実さがなければ、世界は崩壊するだろう。
```

### "take one's time"
**意味**: ゆっくりする、急がない
```
例: Please take your time.
    ゆっくりどうぞ。
```

### "be skeptical"
**意味**: 懐疑的である
```
例: I'm skeptical about fortune tellers.
    占い師については懐疑的です。
```

## 13.10 日本の宗教観を説明する（改善版）

```
Japanese people don't have a specific religion.
日本人は特定の宗教を持っていません。

We believe in many gods.
私たちは多くの神を信じています。

We celebrate Christmas, but we also visit shrines on New Year's Day.
私たちはクリスマスを祝いますが、正月には神社にも参拝します。

It's more of a cultural tradition than a religious practice.
これは宗教的な行為というより、文化的な伝統です。

We pray at shrines when we want to pass exams or find a partner.
試験に受かりたい時や、恋人が欲しい時に神社で祈ります。
```

## 13.14 練習問題 解答

### A. play と pray を正しく使い分けよ
1. I **play** the piano every day.
2. We go to the shrine to **pray**.
3. Let's **play** soccer after school.
4. People **pray** for good luck.

### B. believe / believe in を正しく使い分けよ
1. I **believe** you.
2. I **believe in** God.
3. Do you **believe** fortune tellers?
4. Do you **believe in** ghosts?

### C. 正しい形に直せ
1. They are good at **observing** people.
2. **I was in the same situation before.** / **I experienced the same situation before.**
3. They can **give effective advice.** / They can **advise them very effectively.**
4. I haven't experienced **anything strange.**

### D. 日本語を英語に
1. Have you ever visited a fortune teller?
2. I've had a dream that came true.
3. I'm skeptical about fortune telling.
4. Without honesty, the world would fall apart.

1. **pray と play を区別する** - スペルと使い分けを練習

3. **前置詞の後は動名詞** - good at ~ing, interested in ~ing
4. **advice と advise の区別** - 名詞と動詞
