# Lesson 14: Cultural Exchange【文化交流】

## レッスン概要
- **トピック**: フィリピン料理、旅行経験、趣味、世界平和
- **評価**: C+（改善の兆しあり）
- **主な課題**: live vs leave の混同、疑問文の時制、前置詞の欠如

---

## 14.1 致命的エラー：live vs leave の継続的な混同

```
❌ Bad: "many people want to leave low price"
✅ Good: "many people want to live at a low price" / "live affordably"

❌ Bad: "I live in Sibylla"（地名の発音）
✅ Good: "I live in Shibuya"
```

**再確認**:
- **live** /lɪv/ = 住む、生きる
- **leave** /liːv/ = 去る、出発する

---

## 14.2 致命的エラー：疑問文の時制

```
❌ Bad: "Which part of Philippines have you went to?"
✅ Good: "Which part of the Philippines have you been to?"

❌ Bad: "Why did you went here?"
✅ Good: "Why did you come here?" / "Why did you go there?"

❌ Bad: "I didn't went there"
✅ Good: "I didn't go there"
```

**ルール**:
- have/has の後は**過去分詞** (gone, been, eaten)
- did の後は**原形** (go, come, eat)

| 文型 | 正しい形 |
|------|----------|
| Have you + 過去分詞 | Have you been to...? |
| Did you + 原形 | Did you go to...? |
| I didn't + 原形 | I didn't go there |

## 14.3 重要エラー：前置詞の欠如

```
❌ Bad: "I went to McDonald"
✅ Good: "I went to McDonald's"

❌ Bad: "near sea"
✅ Good: "near the sea"

❌ Bad: "during the childhood"
✅ Good: "during my childhood" / "when I was a child"
```

---

## 14.4 重要エラー：形容詞と比較級

```
❌ Bad: "You are very younger"
✅ Good: "You are very young" / "You have a young hobby"

❌ Bad: "my house is cheaper prefecture"
✅ Good: "my house is in Chiba prefecture"
```

**注意**: younger は比較級なので very は使えない。much younger なら OK。

## 14.8 新出イディオム・表現

### "show someone around"
**意味**: 〜を案内する
```
例: He showed me around Manila.
    彼はマニラを案内してくれた。
```

### "I envy you"
**意味**: うらやましい
```
例: I really envy you because you can swim.
    泳げるなんてうらやましい。
```

### "run out of time"
**意味**: 時間がなくなる
```
例: We are already running out of time.
    もう時間がなくなってきた。
```

### "be interested in"
**意味**: 〜に興味がある
```
例: I should be interested in instruments.
    楽器に興味を持つべきだ。
```

## 14.14 練習問題 解答

### A. 疑問文の時制を正しく直せ
1. Which country **have you been to**?
2. Why **did you go** there?
3. Have you ever **eaten** balut?
4. I **didn't go** to the beach.

### B. 前置詞を入れよ
1. I went **to** McDonald's.
2. I'm interested **in** playing guitar.
3. I'm not good **at** swimming.
4. He showed me **around** the city.

### C. 日本語を英語に
1. Have you ever been to the Philippines?
2. He showed me around Tokyo.
3. I don't know how to swim.
4. It's important to understand different cultures.

### D. 自然な表現に直せ
1. "I got **my open water diving license**"
2. "**snorkeling**"
3. "He **showed me around** Manila"

## 14.15 総合評価

| 項目 | 評価 | コメント |
|------|------|---------|
| live/leave の区別 | D | まだ混同している |
| 疑問文の時制 | D | have + 過去分詞、did + 原形が混乱 |
| 会話力 | B | 話題を広げて楽しく会話できている |
| 語彙力 | B+ | フィリピンの食べ物など覚えている |
| 流暢さ | B | 自然な会話のリズムがある |
| 内容の深さ | B | 世界平和についても意見を述べている |

### 次回までの課題
1. **have + 過去分詞** を徹底する - been, eaten, gone など
2. **did + 原形** を徹底する - went ❌ → go ✅
3. **show someone around** を使う練習
4. **snorkeling** の発音を練習
