## 15.1 重要エラー：rain の正しい使い方

```
❌ Bad: "if it rains hardly"
✅ Good: "if it rains hard" / "if it rains heavily"

❌ Bad: "if it delays hard"
✅ Good: "if it rains hard"
```

**ルール**:
- **rain** は動詞として使う: It rains / It is raining
- **hard** は副詞（hardly ❌）: rain hard = 激しく雨が降る
- **heavily** も使える: rain heavily

| 表現 | 意味 |
|------|------|
| It rains | 雨が降る |
| It rains hard | 激しく雨が降る |
| It rains heavily | 激しく雨が降る |
| heavy rain | 大雨（名詞として） |

**注意**: hardly = ほとんど〜ない（否定の意味）

## 15.2 重要エラー：success vs successful

```
❌ Bad: "I feel successfully"
✅ Good: "I feel successful"

❌ Bad: "success means achieving goals"（文の構造として）
✅ Good: "success means achieving goals" または "being successful means achieving goals"

❌ Bad: "for being successful person"
✅ Good: "for being a successful person"
```

| 単語 | 品詞 | 意味 |
|------|------|------|
| success | 名詞 | 成功 |
| successful | 形容詞 | 成功した |
| successfully | 副詞 | 成功裏に |

## 15.7 新出イディオム・表現

### "deal with"
**意味**: 対処する、扱う
```
例: What kind of weather is most challenging to deal with?
    対処するのが最も難しい天気は何ですか？
```

### "get blamed"
**意味**: 非難される
```
例: Weather forecasters sometimes get blamed for inaccurate forecasts.
    天気予報士は時々、不正確な予報で非難される。
```

### "be patient"
**意味**: 忍耐強い
```
例: Are you patient enough when achieving your goals?
    目標を達成する時、十分に忍耐強いですか？
```

## 15.11 練習問題

### A. 正しい形に直せ

1. If it rains hardly, I will stay home.
2. I feel successfully when I achieve my goals.
3. The liver level is getting high.
4. I catched what you said.

### B. 日本語を英語に

1. 激しく雨が降っている
2. 天気予報は正確ではない
3. 成功とは目標を達成することだ
4. 人脈作りは成功に不可欠だ

### C. success, successful, successfully を使い分けよ

1. I want to be a ___ person.
2. His ___ motivated me.
3. She ___ completed the project.
4. ___ means different things to different people.

---

## 15.12 練習問題 解答

### A. 正しい形に直せ
1. If it rains **hard**, I will stay home.
2. I feel **successful** when I achieve my goals.
3. The **river** level is getting high.
4. I **caught** what you said.

### B. 日本語を英語に
1. It's raining hard. / It's raining heavily.
2. Weather forecasts are not accurate. / Weather forecasting is inaccurate.
3. Success means achieving goals.
4. Networking is essential for success.

### C. success, successful, successfully を使い分けよ
1. I want to be a **successful** person.
2. His **success** motivated me.
3. She **successfully** completed the project.
4. **Success** means different things to different people.
