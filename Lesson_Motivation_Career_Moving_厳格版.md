# PART 1: 繰り返される致命的パターン

## 1.1 時制の混乱【最重要課題】

### 現在完了形の誤用

| あなたの発言 | 問題点 | 正しい表現 |
|-------------|--------|-----------|
| "I've not decided anything" | 短縮形の問題 | "I **haven't** decided on anything" |
| "I've been to many South Asia country" | 単数/複数 | "I've been to many South **Asian countries**" |
| "I've not. I've never been to" | 言い直しの混乱 | "I've never been to Australia" |

### 過去形と現在形の混同

| あなたの発言 | 問題点 | 正しい表現 |
|-------------|--------|-----------|
| "I became Software Engineer for three years just now" | 時制が混乱 | "I've **been** a software engineer for three years" |
| "it gave me, gives me" | 言い直し | "It **gives** me interesting information" |
| "I used to, used to doing my work" | 混乱 | "I'm **used to** my work" / "I've **gotten used to** my work" |

### 「〜年間」の表現

```
❌ Bad: "I became Software Engineer for three years just now"
✅ Good: "I've been a software engineer for three years now"

❌ Bad: "for a long time, three years, I'm 25 did my university"
✅ Good: "I've been working for three years. I'm 25, and I graduated from university."
```

**ルール**: 「〜年間やっている」は **have been ~ing** または **have + 過去分詞** で表す

---

## 1.2 used to の致命的混同

### 3つの異なる意味

| 表現 | 意味 | 例文 |
|------|------|------|
| **used to + 動詞** | 昔は〜した（過去の習慣） | I used to play baseball. |
| **be used to + 名詞/動名詞** | 〜に慣れている | I'm used to working hard. |
| **get used to + 名詞/動名詞** | 〜に慣れる | I'm getting used to my new job. |

### あなたの間違い

```
❌ Bad: "I used to, used to doing my work. So it's unmotivated"
意図: 「仕事に慣れてしまったからモチベーションが下がった」

✅ Good: "I've gotten used to my work, so I'm feeling unmotivated"
または: "My work has become routine, so I'm not as motivated anymore"
```

## 2.1 不自然な表現

---

## 2.2 言い直し・言い淀みの問題

以下のような発言パターンは理解を困難にする：

### 例1: 時制の混乱
```
❌ "I became Software Engineer for three years just now, and AI technology
    and some AI information evolving every day. So it motivates me and it
    gave it, gave me, gives me some information"

✅ "I've been a software engineer for three years. AI technology is evolving
    every day, which keeps me motivated and interested."
```

### 例2: 構造崩壊
```
❌ "for a long time, three years, I'm 25 did my university after I graduated university"

✅ "I've been working for three years. I'm 25 years old. I graduated from university."
```

### 例3: 複雑な言い回しの失敗
```
❌ "most important thing for work is who entry? Who? Fluently working"

✅ "The most important thing at work is efficiency."
```

**対策**:
- **1文を短くしろ**
- **言い直す前に一呼吸置け**
- **主語→動詞→目的語 の順序を守れ**

# 最後に厳しく言う

## あなたの問題の本質

1. **時制が安定しない**
   - 現在形、過去形、現在完了形が混在
   - 「3年間やっている」= have been ~ing を徹底

2. **冠詞を忘れる**
   - 「a software engineer」の a が必須
   - 日本語にないから意識しにくいが、必ず付ける

3. **言い直しが多すぎる**
   - "gave it, gave me, gives me" のような発言は聞き手に負担
   - 話す前に文を組み立てろ

4. **used to の混同**
   - 3パターンを完璧に区別できていない
   - これは中級者への必須スキル

## 具体的なアクション

1. **この教材の問題5を毎日やれ**（used to の練習）
2. **「I've been ~ for ~ years」を毎日10回言え**
3. **国名は正式名称で言え**（Taiwanese → Taiwan）

**次回レッスンで同じミスをするな。**
